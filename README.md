# Employee Exit Survey Analysis (Power BI)

This project focuses on analyzing the Employee Exit Survey data from the Department of Education, Training and Employment (DETE), Queensland Government. The goal of the analysis is to understand why employees leave, identify patterns in turnover, and provide insights for improving retention and workplace satisfaction.

## ğŸ” Key Insights

- Employees with **less than 4 years of service** showed a higher tendency to leave  
- **Workplace dissatisfaction** (especially workload & management) was a major factor  
- Older employees reported more **health-related resignations**  
- Teaching staff had **higher resignation rates** compared to administrative roles  
- Satisfaction scores decreased consistently in the years leading to resignation
